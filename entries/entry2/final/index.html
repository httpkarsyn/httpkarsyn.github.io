mismatched

i realized we werent meant to be

one year before we went our separate ways

but, thats how long

i tried to change the ending.

i poured love into us,

tried to tip the scales in our favor,

said, look at all these wonderful things about us.

look at how we laugh at all the same jokes

look at how we try to love one another

and how we want the best for each other:

fate shook her head, took my hand in hers and said-

but look at how youre straining in your own skin,

look at how you bite your tongue everyday,

look at all the distance thats already there,

look at how it grows.

but we love each other, i said.

fate sighed,

my dear,

no amount of love

can make two mismatched pieces fit together

not unless,

you mutilate them into something they never were.
