i let go because you shouldnt have to work
so hard for something that is meant to be

i always had a fear that life would divide us,
but in the end it was us
that didnt know how to keep it together.

i guess love isnt enough
when everything else shows up, too

i wont forget

when everything felt right

and i will remember the way we were
before it all changed.
sometimes i wonder

am i free

or am i lonely
